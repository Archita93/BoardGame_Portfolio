Introduction

Coquitlam Crunch draws inspiration from classic racing board games like Snakes and Ladders but with a unique twist inspired by the actual Coquitlam Crunch hike. In this multiplayer game, players race towards the end of the board, simulating the exhilaration and challenges of the real-life hike. The game incorporates elements such as encountering wildlife, strategically managing stamina at designated stations, and facing the unpredictability of the trail.

Using dice to determine their movement, players must navigate the board while being rewarded or penalised based on their choices and the chance events they encounter. Coquitlam Crunch combines the excitement of a race with the thrill of hiking, offering players an immersive and competitive experience in the natural beauty of the city's hiking trails.

Visual Gallery

The board has 92 spots. There are 3 terrains: flat concrete at the start which require 0 stamina tokens; Inclined gravel, which require 1 stamina token per turn; Steep stairs which require 2 stamina tokens per turn. You can rest (skip a turn) to recover 1 stamina token. The game includes player tokens (representing each player), event cards and sabotage cards. In the game, there are stamina stations that you can land on to gain 2 stamina tokens, or one stamina token when you pass it. There are blank spots, random event spots (Which can include picking up a sabotage card or moving up or down or gaining stamina tokens or more), and rare sabotage spots where you can pick up a stamina card.
